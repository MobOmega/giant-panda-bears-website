What Are Pandas?

The Giant Panda (or Panda, or Panda Bear), with the scientific name A. melanoleuca, is a bear species that lives in Asia, and more specifically China. They are considered a vulnerable species as I'm making this. They coexist with many other species. Today I'm going to be teaching you about them.
